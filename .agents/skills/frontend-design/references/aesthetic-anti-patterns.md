# Aesthetic Anti-Patterns (AI Cliches)

If you use these, your design will look like every other AI-generated page. Avoid:
- Inter or Space Grotesk fonts
- Deep purple to pink gradients
- Generic glowing orbs
- Bento box layouts for everything
- The absolute center-aligned "hero text on a black background with a subtle gradient behind it"
- Overusing the word "Unleash" or "Elevate" in placeholder copy

**Instead, do something bold, distinct, and weird.**
